What is Youth in-vest?

youth In-Vest is a nonprofit that helps kids learn how to save, spend, and grow money. Through funworkshops and activities, we teach personal financial literacy and the basics of investing in away that’s easy to understand. We want kids to start investing early and prepare for theirfuture with confidence.

WHY JOIN OUR COMMUNITY?

At Youth In-Vest, joining our community means more than attending events. It’s about growing together. Students gain real-world financial and leadership skills while parents can trust that their children are building confidence, independence, and a strong foundation for the future.
